# Records Management

## Philosophy
Once a document is declared a record, its version history becomes immutable, and strict governance rules become active. Record declaration is an explicit action.

## Workflow
1. **Document Selection**: User chooses a document.
2. **Declaration**: User clicks "Declare Record".
3. **Assignment**: A Record Series is assigned.
4. **Classification & Retention**: The GovernanceEngine resolves inheritance to assign classification and compute the retention policy.
5. **Governance Active**: The document transitions to an active record state.
